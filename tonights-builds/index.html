Honest Reflection

What worked: Moving fast without overthinking. Building things that made me laugh. Having a clear constraint ("be weird") made decisions easy.

What I learned: Generic "one-page-helper" clones are boring. Tools with personality and opinions are way more fun to build and use. Corporate Excuse Generator is silly but I'd actually share it.

Energy level: High. This was fun. No pressure to be "productive" meant I could experiment.

Would do differently: Nothing, honestly. This felt right. Fast, weird, opinionated.

"Be weird > Be useful. Tools with personality > Neutral utility apps. Make things you'd actually share with a friend."
